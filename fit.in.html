What is Fitness

      1.Fitness is a very personal term! Fitness is is having a healthy mind, body, and spirit to allow you to maximize your potential and help others maximize their potential. Your definition of fitness will
be influenced by your interests, physical abilities, and goals. Being physically fit and healthy involves having a fit: 1. Mind 2. Body 3. Spirit

     2.Fitness for the average person might be the ability to perform functional activities required in his or her daily routine. Athletes have a higher level of fitness requirement in order to perform their sport. In general, muscle strength, endurance, flexibility, nutrition, and mental health are all crucial in fitness.
